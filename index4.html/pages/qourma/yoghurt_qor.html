How We Created It

We cooked onions until golden, added garlic, ginger, and ground spices, then slowly mixed in
whisked yogurt to form a creamy base. Simmered gently until thick and flavorful, finished with coriander and a touch of garam masala.

  • Coriander & cumin powder

  • Garam masala
