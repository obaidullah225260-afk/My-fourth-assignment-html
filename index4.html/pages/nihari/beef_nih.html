Beef Nihari is a slow-cooked stew famous in Pakistan. It has tender beef shank meat simmered for hours
with wheat flour and rich spices, giving a deep flavor and thick gravy. Usually enjoyed with naan or paratha.

How We Created It

We slow-cooked beef with bones in a blend of spices, added fried onions and wheat flour to thicken,
and let it simmer until the meat became fall-apart tender. Served hot with lemon, green chilies, and ginger slices.

Ingredients

  • Beef shank (with bone marrow)
  • Onions
  • Wheat flour
  • Ginger & garlic
  • Yogurt
  • Whole spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt
  • Fresh coriander & ginger slices
