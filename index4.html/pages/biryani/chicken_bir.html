Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken – 1 kg
  • Basmati rice – ½ kg
  • Yogurt – 1 cup
  • Tomatoes – 2 medium (chopped)
  • Onions – 3 large (sliced & fried golden)
  • Ginger garlic paste – 2 tbsp
  • Green chilies – 3–4
  • Biryani masala – 2 tbsp
  • Red chili powder – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric – ½ tsp
  • Garam masala – ½ tsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Oil/ghee – ½ cup
  • Fresh coriander & mint leaves – handful each
  • Saffron or food color – 2 pinches (in milk)

Steps:

  1. Wash rice & soak for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, salt, and red chili powder.
  3. Cook chicken with tomatoes until tender.
  4. Boil rice with salt & whole spices (cloves, cardamom, bay leaf).
  5. Layer half rice, then chicken masala, fried onions, mint, coriander, saffron milk → repeat layers.
  6. Cover & cook on low flame (dum) for 15–20 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with raita or salad.
