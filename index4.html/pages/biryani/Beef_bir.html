Beef biryani!

Beef Biryani is a rich and flavorful South Asian dish made with tender beef, aromatic basmati rice, and a blend of traditional spices.
It is known for its bold taste, unique layers, and festive aroma that makes it a favorite for gatherings and special occasions.

How we created??!!

We created this Beef Biryani by marinating the beef in spices, slow-cooking it until tender, and then layering it with fragrant basmati rice.
Each layer is infused with saffron and herbs to bring out the perfect balance of flavor and aroma.

Recipe!

  • 1 kg beef (with bones)
  • 3 cups basmati rice
  • 2 large onions (fried golden brown)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 3-4 green chilies
  • Whole spices (cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, bay leaf)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk (or yellow food color)
  • Cooking oil / ghee
  • Salt to taste
