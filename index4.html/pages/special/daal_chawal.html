How We Created It

We boiled yellow lentils until soft, added a spicy tarka of onions, garlic, tomatoes, and green chilies, then poured
it over hot steamed rice. Served with achar, salad, or papad for the true desi taste.

Ingredients

  • Yellow lentils (moong/masoor)
  • Onion & tomato
  • Garlic & ginger
  • Green chilies
  • Turmeric & red chili powder
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee
  • Steamed rice
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)
