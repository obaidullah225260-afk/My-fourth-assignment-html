Chicken karahi

Chicken Karahi is a classic Pakistani and North Indian dish cooked in a wok-like pan called “Karahi.”
It is famous for its spicy, tangy tomato-based gravy, tender chicken, and aroma of freshly ground spices.

How we created!!??

We created this Chicken Karahi by stir-frying chicken pieces in oil with ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Then we added fresh tomatoes and spices, cooking it until the chicken absorbed the flavors.
Garnished with coriander and ginger, it’s served hot with naan or roti.

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into medium pieces)
  • 4-5 medium tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 medium onions (optional, finely sliced)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 3-4 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp crushed coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp black pepper (freshly crushed)
  • Fresh ginger (julienned) for garnish
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Cooking oil / ghee
  • Salt to taste
