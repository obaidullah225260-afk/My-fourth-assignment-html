Pepperoni Pizza is one of the most popular pizzas worldwide. It has a thin crust base with tangy tomato
sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and spicy pepperoni slices baked to perfection.

How We Created It

We spread tomato sauce over fresh pizza dough,added a thick layer of mozzarella cheese,
topped it with pepperoni slices, and baked until golden with crispy edges and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil
  • Dried oregano & chili flakes
