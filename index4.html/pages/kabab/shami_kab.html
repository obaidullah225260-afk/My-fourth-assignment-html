Shami Kabab is a traditional Pakistani-Indian patty made with minced meat, lentils, and spices. Soft inside,
crispy outside ‚Äî perfect with paratha, chutney, or as a snack in burgers and rolls.

How We Created It

We boiled minced meat with chana daal, ginger, garlic, and whole spices until tender. Then ground it to a smooth paste,
shaped round patties, and shallow fried until golden brown. Served with mint chutney and onions.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef or chicken
  • Chana daal (split Bengal gram)
  • Onion
  • Garlic & ginger
  • Green chilies
  • Whole spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • Egg (for binding)
  • Salt & red chili powder
  • Oil (for frying)
